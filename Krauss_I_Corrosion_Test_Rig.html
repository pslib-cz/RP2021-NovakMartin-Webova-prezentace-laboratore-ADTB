Krauss I – Corrosion Test Rig

Aplication

Testing in corrosive environment

Parameters

Controlled value : torque, pressure
Torque : max ±750 Nm
Pressure : max 250 bar
Rotation speed : 660 rpm
Motor Power : 55kW
Corrosive conditions : salt spray and fog, water humidity, salt solution coating
Provides : Krauss corrosion test, Stiction test
