FB Jablonec – Dynamometer 6

Aplication

Test Rig for semi dynamic endurance tests

Parameters

Tested parts : Disc brake & Drum brake assemblies
Control system : Horiba STARTS
Control loop - Service brake : Braking Pressure, Braking Torque
Control loop - Hand brake : Cable Force, Current

Testing parameters

Maximal Dynamic Torque : 5500 Nm (7000 Nm with overload)
Maximal Static Torque : 5000 Nm
Maximal Pressure : 250 bar
Maximal Speed : 2200 rpm
Minimal Inertia : 20 kg•m2
Maximal Inertia : 100 kg•m2 (250 kgm2 with Inertia Simulation)
Inertia Increments : Continuous through Inertia-Simulation
Main drive motor : 220 kW

Measurement system

Temperature : 4 x 0-1000°C 2 x 0-1000°C signal transmition from rotating disc
Torque : 5500 Nm
Sample rate : 2000 Hz
Fluid Volume : VSE flow meter
Deflection : 24 x Lasers

Cooling Air System

Air Volume : 3000 m3/h with AC or 4500 m3/h with blower
Air Temperature : -20 to 50 °C
Air Humidity : 15 to 90%
